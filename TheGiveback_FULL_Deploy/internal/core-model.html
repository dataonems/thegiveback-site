At $1M monthly volume, the dual pricing model generates $18,000 in margin. Your 30% covenant routes $5,400 directly to vetted Colombian foundations — automatically, from every transaction. The referral partner earns $3,600 for bringing the merchant. You retain $8,820 for operations. That is the entire model. One merchant. One million dollars. One covenant.

Assumptions: Dual pricing model at 1.8% blended margin. No deductions for interchange, network fees, or processing costs — those are already reflected in the dual pricing structure. Referral partner rate and covenant rate are both adjustable above. Partner donation is always voluntary — never required. This is the simplified Phase 1 model. Academy, Live Events, Giveback Card, and marketplace revenue are not included here — they are Phase 3+ features that compound on top of this foundation.
