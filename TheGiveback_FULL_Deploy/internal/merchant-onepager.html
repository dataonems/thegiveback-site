Our promises to every certified merchant
  • Your rates and your customers' experience never change. Certification costs you nothing.
  • The giving comes from our earnings. Never from your residuals and never hidden in your processing fees.
  • Every dollar is publicly traceable — from your transaction volume to the foundation's receipt.
  • A small shop's giveback has identical dignity to a large one's. We never tier generosity by size.
  • Your default cause is hyper-local to your zip code and business type before anything global.
